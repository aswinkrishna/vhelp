About Us

Emirates Quotation is an online marketplace that provides quotes and online bookings for moving, home services, and insurance through an extensive network of more than 200 partners. On Emirates Quotation, customers can read detailed service offerings and user reviews of each provider before selecting which company to use.

Founded in Dubai in 2013, Emirates Quotation has grown to four cities in the Middle East, with plans to expand in the MENA region and beyond.
